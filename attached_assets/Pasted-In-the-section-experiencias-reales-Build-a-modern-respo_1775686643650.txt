In the section “experiencias reales” Build a modern, responsive testimonial section for a luxury nautical experiences website called ORSA, focused on exclusivity, emotions, and premium service.
The goal is to make users feel that this is not just a boat ride, but a memorable, high-end experience on the water in Guatapé.

SECTION STRUCTURE:
1. Top banner (highlight strip)
* Full-width section with a premium warm gradient (dark blue to gold accent or soft sunset tones)
* Centered bold headline: "Muchos llegan por el paisaje… pero se quedan por la experiencia."
* Subtext below with a subtle icon: "Momentos únicos, conexiones reales y recuerdos que se quedan contigo."

1. Reviews section title
* Large, bold, centered heading: "EXPERIENCIAS DE NUESTROS CLIENTES"

1. Testimonial section (main content)
* Full-width background image or video of a real ORSA experience: (people on a yacht, wakeboard, sunset in Guatapé, celebration on boat)
* Overlay a semi-transparent dark luxury card (glass effect)
    * Positioned right on desktop
    * Full width on mobile

1. Testimonial card content:
* Name + context (e.g. "Camila & amigos", "Pareja - Medellín", "Grupo internacional")
* 1–2 paragraphs of testimonial text:
    * Describe the experience (service, boat, activities)
    * Highlight premium details (music, drinks, attention)
    * Emotional result (exclusivity, connection, unforgettable moment)
Example tone: "Desde que llegamos todo fue perfecto. La atención, la música, el ambiente… se siente como una experiencia privada de lujo. Definitivamente repetiríamos."
* Optional elegant emojis (🌅 🚤 ✨) but minimal

1. Design requirements:
* Clean, modern, luxury UI
* Rounded corners (2xl)
* Soft shadows and glassmorphism effect
* Elegant spacing and layout
* Smooth transitions and hover effects
* Mobile-first responsive design
* Typography:
    * Serif font for titles (luxury feel)
    * Sans-serif (Inter or Poppins) for body

1. Optional enhancements:
* Add carousel functionality (horizontal scroll or slider-ready)
* Subtle fade-in animation on load
* Parallax effect on background image
* Include small avatar or circular image for testimonial author

GOAL: Create a visually stunning, emotionally engaging testimonial section that builds trust, reinforces exclusivity, and motivates users to book a premium experience with ORSA.
